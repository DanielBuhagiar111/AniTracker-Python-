Welcome to my anime search engine the following indicate how it works:
1. First once logged in go on the input which has the search icon button next to it.
2. Then in this input field type the anime title or synopsis of the anime you wish to view.
3. Dont worry if you cant remember it exactly the search will still work.
Note: Avoid using words like 'and', 'after' and 'a' as they will be ignored.
4. When your done press the search button and you will be taken to a new page,
in this page the 3 most similar animes to your search will be outputted.
5. Then click on the anime you wanted and you will be taken to its corresponding page.
6. On the corresponding page you can see the anime title and synopsis, the average
score given by all the users and if already inputted your score.
7. If you have not entered your score yet you can input it in the input field.
8. When you are done from viewing this page you can either sign out or go back to the
search page to search for another anime. Make sure to log out or youll stay logged in!
